Presentation

I work as a researcher at EDF Lab Saclay near Paris. My activity is centered on the modelling of energy markets : hedging, risk computations, price models.

Before, I have prepared a PhD at the ENS Paris Saclay an CEA on modelling wave propagation through random media using uncertainty quantification techniques. My manuscript can be found here.

I also give lectures at ENSTA Paris in Probability and Statistics.
